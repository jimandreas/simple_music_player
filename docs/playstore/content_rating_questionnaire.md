# Content Rating Questionnaire Guide

This document provides recommended answers for the Google Play Console content rating questionnaire.

## App Category
**Primary Category:** Music & Audio

## Violence
- **Does the app contain violence?** No
- **Does the app depict violence against characters?** No
- **Does the app contain graphic violence?** No
- **Does the app contain violence toward real people?** No

## Sexuality
- **Does the app contain sexual content?** No
- **Does the app contain nudity?** No
- **Does the app contain sexual themes?** No

## Language
- **Does the app contain profanity or crude humor?** No
- **Does the app allow users to interact or exchange content?** No

## Controlled Substances
- **Does the app reference or depict the use of drugs, alcohol, or tobacco?** No

## Gambling
- **Does the app simulate gambling?** No
- **Does the app contain real gambling?** No

## User-Generated Content
- **Does the app contain user-generated content?** No
- **Does the app allow users to communicate?** No

## Location Sharing
- **Does the app share user location with other users?** No

## Miscellaneous
- **Does the app contain any content that could be disturbing to users?** No
- **Is the app a browser or search engine?** No

## In-App Purchases
- **Does the app contain in-app purchases?** No

## Ads
- **Does the app contain ads?** No

## Expected Rating

Based on these answers, the app should receive:
- **ESRB:** Everyone (E)
- **PEGI:** 3
- **USK:** 0
- **GRAC:** All
- **IARC:** 3+

## Notes for Submission

This is a simple music player that:
1. Only plays local audio files from user-selected folders
2. Has no internet connectivity requirements
3. Has no social features
4. Has no monetization
5. Contains no objectionable content

The app should qualify for the lowest content rating in all regions.
