## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Music Player ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Music Player is designed with privacy in mind. The app:
- Does not collect personal data
- Does not track user behavior
- Does not use analytics services
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not require an internet connection
- Does not transmit any data to external servers

## Permissions

Music Player requires the following permissions to function:

### Storage Access (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)
- **Purpose:** To read audio files from folders you select on your device
- **Usage:** Files are read locally and played using your device's media player
- **Data Handling:** File data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted externally

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device:
- Selected folder preferences
- Playback state

This data remains on your device and is not backed up to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

## Children's Privacy

Music Player does not collect information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.
