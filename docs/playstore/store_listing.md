# Google Play Store Listing

## App Name
Music Player

## Short Description (80 characters max)
Simple folder-based music player. Play MP3, FLAC, WAV & more from any folder.

## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Enjoy your music collection with this clean, lightweight music player. Simply select a folder containing your audio files and start listening immediately.

**Key Features:**

• Folder-based playback - Select any folder on your device and play all audio files within it
• Wide format support - MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A, and other common audio formats
• Intuitive controls - Play, pause, skip to previous/next track with ease
• Volume control - Adjust playback volume directly in the app
• Shuffle mode - Randomize your playlist for a fresh listening experience
• Seek bar - Jump to any position in the current track
• Now Playing view - Always see what's currently playing
• Auto-scroll - Automatically scrolls to the current track in your list

**Why Choose This Music Player?**

• No bloat - Does one thing and does it well
• No ads - Enjoy uninterrupted music
• Privacy focused - No data collection, no tracking
• Modern design - Clean Material 3 interface
• Lightweight - Minimal battery and storage usage

**How It Works:**

1. Tap "Select Folder" to choose a folder containing your music
2. Your audio files appear in a scrollable list
3. Tap any track to start playing
4. Use the playback controls to manage your listening

Perfect for users who organize their music in folders and want a simple, no-frills player that just works.

**Supported Formats:**
MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, M4A, AAC, and other formats supported by Android MediaPlayer.

**Permissions Explained:**
• Storage access: Required to read your audio files from selected folders
